# Shipping Rates

Shipping rates describe the price of shipping presented to your customers and applied to a purchase. For more information, see [Charge for shipping](https://docs.stripe.com/docs/payments/during-payment/charge-shipping.md).

## Endpoints

### Create a shipping rate

- [POST /v1/shipping_rates](https://docs.stripe.com/api/shipping_rates/create.md)

### Update a shipping rate

- [POST /v1/shipping_rates/:id](https://docs.stripe.com/api/shipping_rates/update.md)

### Retrieve a shipping rate

- [GET /v1/shipping_rates/:id](https://docs.stripe.com/api/shipping_rates/retrieve.md)

### List all shipping rates

- [GET /v1/shipping_rates](https://docs.stripe.com/api/shipping_rates/list.md)
