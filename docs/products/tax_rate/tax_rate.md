# Tax Rate

Tax rates can be applied to [invoices](https://docs.stripe.com/invoicing/taxes/tax-rates.md), [subscriptions](https://docs.stripe.com/billing/taxes/tax-rates.md) and [Checkout Sessions](https://docs.stripe.com/payments/checkout/use-manual-tax-rates.md) to collect tax.

Related guide: [Tax rates](https://docs.stripe.com/billing/taxes/tax-rates.md)

## Endpoints

### Create a tax rate

- [POST /v1/tax_rates](https://docs.stripe.com/api/tax_rates/create.md)

### Update a tax rate

- [POST /v1/tax_rates/:id](https://docs.stripe.com/api/tax_rates/update.md)

### Retrieve a tax rate

- [GET /v1/tax_rates/:id](https://docs.stripe.com/api/tax_rates/retrieve.md)

### List all tax rates

- [GET /v1/tax_rates](https://docs.stripe.com/api/tax_rates/list.md)
