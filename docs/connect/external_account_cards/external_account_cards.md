### Create a card

### Update a card

- [POST /v1/accounts/:id/external_accounts/:id](https://docs.stripe.com/api/external_account_cards/update.md)

### Retrieve a card

- [GET /v1/accounts/:id/external_accounts/:id](https://docs.stripe.com/api/external_account_cards/retrieve.md)

### List all cards

- [GET /v1/accounts/:id/external_accounts](https://docs.stripe.com/api/external_account_cards/list.md)

### Delete a card

- [DELETE /v1/accounts/:id/external_accounts/:id](https://docs.stripe.com/api/external_account_cards/delete.md)
