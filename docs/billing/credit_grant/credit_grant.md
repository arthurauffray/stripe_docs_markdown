# Credit Grant

A credit grant is an API resource that documents the allocation of some billing credits to a customer.

Related guide: [Billing credits](https://docs.stripe.com/billing/subscriptions/usage-based/billing-credits.md)

## Endpoints

### Create a credit grant

- [POST /v1/billing/credit_grants](https://docs.stripe.com/api/billing/credit-grant/create.md)

### Update a credit grant

- [POST /v1/billing/credit_grants/:id](https://docs.stripe.com/api/billing/credit-grant/update.md)

### Retrieve a credit grant

- [GET /v1/billing/credit_grants/:id](https://docs.stripe.com/api/billing/credit-grant/retrieve.md)

### List credit grants

- [GET /v1/billing/credit_grants](https://docs.stripe.com/api/billing/credit-grant/list.md)

### Expire a credit grant

- [POST /v1/billing/credit_grants/:id/expire](https://docs.stripe.com/api/billing/credit-grant/expire.md)

### Void a credit grant

- [POST /v1/billing/credit_grants/:id/void](https://docs.stripe.com/api/billing/credit-grant/void.md)
