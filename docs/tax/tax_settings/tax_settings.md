# Tax Settings

You can use Tax `Settings` to manage configurations used by Stripe Tax calculations.

Related guide: [Using the Settings API](https://docs.stripe.com/docs/tax/settings-api.md)

## Endpoints

### Update settings

- [POST /v1/tax/settings](https://docs.stripe.com/api/tax/settings/update.md)

### Retrieve settings

- [GET /v1/tax/settings](https://docs.stripe.com/api/tax/settings/retrieve.md)
